# Objectives

## Overall Objective

AI-NTD aims to develop an integrated artificial intelligence platform for accelerating drug discovery targeting neglected tropical diseases (NTDs), with a focus on Chagas disease and Leishmaniasis.

## Specific Objectives

### Objective 1: Build a multi-modal molecular representation learning framework
Develop a graph neural network architecture that integrates molecular structure, protein target information, and phenotypic screening data to predict compound activity against NTD targets.

### Objective 2: Create a validated virtual screening pipeline
Design and validate an AI-driven virtual screening pipeline capable of processing 10M+ compounds against 5 key NTD targets, benchmarked against experimental high-throughput screening results.

### Objective 3: Identify and validate lead compounds
Use the platform to identify 50 candidate compounds, synthesize the top 20, and validate activity in cell-based assays for Chagas disease (T. cruzi) and Leishmaniasis (L. donovani).

### Objective 4: Establish an open-access NTD compound database
Create a publicly accessible database of AI-predicted compound-target interactions for the NTD research community, integrated with existing resources (ChEMBL, PubChem).
