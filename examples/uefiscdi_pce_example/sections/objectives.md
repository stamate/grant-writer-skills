# Objectives

## General Objective

To develop novel graph neural network architectures for accurate prediction of protein-ligand binding affinity, advancing computational drug discovery methodology.

## Specific Objectives

1. **Design equivariant GNN architectures** for molecular interaction modeling that respect the 3D rotational symmetry of protein-ligand complexes.

2. **Benchmark against state-of-the-art** methods (RF-Score, OnionNet, PIGNet) on PDBbind v2020 and CASF-2016 datasets, targeting Pearson correlation > 0.85.

3. **Validate on real-world drug discovery tasks** by collaborating with the Institute of Organic Chemistry "C.D. Nenitzescu" to test predictions against experimental binding data for kinase inhibitors.
